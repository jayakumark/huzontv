huzon.tv Facebook App Support

Even though huzon.tv is not a public app -- the only users should be reporters at huzon.tv client TV stations -- Facebook requires a publicly-published support page to conform to their guidelines.

huzon.tv provides direct phone and/or email support for any issues you (the station or reporter) may have. Please us the contact us page to send an email or contact your station's marketing manager, internet sales director or news director to get in touch with huzon.tv support staff. Thanks!
