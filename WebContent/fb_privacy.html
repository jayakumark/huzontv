huzon.tv Facebook App Privacy Policy

Even though huzon.tv is not a public app -- the only users should be reporters at huzon.tv client TV stations -- Facebook requires a public privacy policy page to conform to their guidelines.

To this end, the huzon.tv Facebook app does only the following: It automatically posts frame grabs on behalf of reporters when they appear on TV. This lets their followers know when to tune in to the TV station's broadcast or live stream.

Occasionally, if an automated post is incorrect or needs to be taken down for some reason, huzon.tv staff may delete the automated post.

huzon.tv does not read or store any personal information about the reporter.
