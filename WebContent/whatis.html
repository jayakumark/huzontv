What is huzon.tv?

Automated social marketing for local TV

huzon.tv is a 100% automated social media marketing system for local TV stations. In short, it watches your station's channel and looks for your reporters' faces. When it sees them, huzon.tv Tweets and/or Facebooks the image from their accounts along with a link to the station's live streaming video feed.

What's the goal?

Each of your reporters has a relatively loyal follower base on social media. huzon.tv is designed to supplement his/her normal postings with compelling, actionable social activity complete with rich imagery from and direct links to the newscast.

100% automated? Really?

Yes. Our leadership team has years of experience developing products for local TV stations. We know that there is no manpower to spare on any recurring tasks. Thus, full automation was our primary design criterion.

How does it integrate with my station's existing systems?

It doesn't! huzon.tv only needs to capture your OTA broadcast. Bandwidth usage is ~250 kbps (.25 mbps).

Does huzon.tv record and/or publish video?

No. huzon.tv is not a video platform. It is designed to send viewers to your TV station's existing broadcast, cablecast, live Internet stream, mobile application or any other destination you choose.

How customizable is the system?

huzon.tv enables its partner stations to choose which reporters' accounts are connected to the system, how often they trigger alerts and what the alert messages say.

How does it work?

At a high level, huzon.tv does the following during the life-cycle of an automated post:
1. Identify reporter - Uses face recognition to spot your on-air personalities.
2. Fire alert - Uses social APIs create posts on their social channels (Twitter & Facebook)
3. Steer traffic - Captures clickthroughs and sends users to the desired destination
