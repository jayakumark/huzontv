huzon.tv Facebook App Terms of Service

Even though huzon.tv is not a public app -- the only users should be reporters at huzon.tv client TV stations -- Facebook requires a public terms of service page to conform to their guidelines.

By enabling/installing the app, the user (reporter) agrees to allow huzon.tv to automatically post images and text on their pages, stream and/or timeline when they appear on TV and delete these automated postings for any reason.

The huzon.tv app will not do anything other than that which is stated above.
